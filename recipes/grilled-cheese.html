Tortilla Soup

Description

My childhood favorite. Can't go wrong with grilled cheese. Usually. I left a lot of room for error with these directions. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 oz cheese
  • 2-3 tsp butter

Steps

  1. Heat the pan
  2. Butter the bread
  3. Put cheese on the bread
  4. Put sandwich on the pan
